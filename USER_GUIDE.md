# FocusFlow: The Ultimate Productivity Guide

Welcome to **FocusFlow**, your gamified companion for deep work and peak productivity. This guide covers every feature of the application to help you master your time and reach your goals.

---

## 🕒 1. The Core Timer
The heart of FocusFlow is the **Pomodoro Timer**, designed to keep you focused and refreshed.

- **Three Modes**:
    - **Pomodoro**: 25-minute deep work session (Customizable).
    - **Short Break**: 5-minute quick refresh (Customizable).
    - **Long Break**: 15-minute extended recovery (Customizable).
- **Task Integration**: Select a task from your list to stay focused on a specific goal.
- **Audio Feedback**: Relaxing bell, chime, or beep sounds notify you when a session ends.
- **Auto-Start**: Optionally enable "Auto-start Breaks" in settings to keep the flow going without manual intervention.

---

## ✅ 2. Task Management
Stay organized by tracking exactly what you need to accomplish.

- **Quick Add**: Create tasks with titles and categories.
- **Status Tracking**: Mark tasks as complete directly from the dashboard.
- **Focus Mode**: Bind a task to your current timer session to track focus time per task.

---

## 🎮 3. Gamification & Progression
FocusFlow turns productivity into a game to keep you motivated.

### **XP & Levels**
- **Earn XP**: Gain 25 XP for every Pomodoro session completed.
- **Level Up**: Progress through 10 levels of mastery. Your current level is displayed with a progress ring on your profile.

### **Weekly Leagues**
Compete with your past self and move up the ranks based on your weekly focus:
- **Bronze**: Entry level.
- **Silver**: 500+ Weekly XP.
- **Gold**: 1,500+ Weekly XP.
- **Platinum**: 3,500+ Weekly XP.

### **Achievements**
Unlock 10 unique badges with increasing difficulty:
1.  **First Step**: 10 Pomodoros.
2.  **Getting Hot**: 100 Pomodoros.
3.  **Diamond Mind**: 1,000 Pomodoros.
4.  **The Monk**: 2,500 Pomodoros.
5.  **Night Owl**: 300 sessions after 10 PM.
6.  **Early Bird**: 400 sessions before 7 AM.
7.  **Week Warrior**: 30-day streak.
8.  **Inferno**: 100-day streak.
9.  **Weekend Grind**: 50 weekend sessions.
10. **Perfectionist**: 70 hours of focus in a single week.

### **Streaks & Freezes**
- **Daily Streak**: Complete at least one Pomodoro a day to build your streak.
- **Streak Freeze**: You start with 2 freezes to protect your streak on days you can't focus. Use them wisely!

## 📊 4. Analytics & History
Gain insights into your work habits.

- **Session History**: View a detailed list of every focus session you've completed.
- **Productivity Trends**: See your total focus minutes and session counts.
- **Heatmap**: Track your consistency over the weeks and months.
- **Leaderboard**: See how you stack up against other FocusFlow users.

## 🎨 5. Personalization
Make the app yours with custom themes and preferences.

- **Themes**:
    - **Default**: Clean, professional light mode.
    - **Dark**: Sleek, high-contrast dark mode for late-night sessions.
    - **Ocean**: Calming blue tones for a relaxed environment.
- **Timer Customization**: Set your preferred duration for Pomodoros and breaks.
